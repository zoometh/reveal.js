Hypersectral analysis and semantisation of the Roca dels Moros (Lleida, Spain)

Thomas Huet University of Oxford
Claudia Sciuto Università di Pisa

Analysis We propose a study of the Roca dels Moros with a characterization of the painting technique and a characterization of the iconographic content of the panel. The painting techniques will be characterized by hyperspectral and pXRF analysis. The hyperspectral analysis will characterize the chemical signature of the paintings. The pXRF is a complementary solution…. Macro photos will be taken with a DinoLite (x20-x200) useful for superimpostions The iconography will be characterized using the model of the icon R package which allows at the same time the spatial distribution, the classification in standardized categories, and the analysis of the shapes. These different results will be confronted to each other by a multifactorial analysis (MFA). Outputs Our approach is clearly oriented towrd Open Science If we are able to recognize statistical regularities in the technical and iconographic signatures, we aim to publish them in a scientific journal. The workflow including data collection, data description, data storage, etc., in a word the DMP, as well as the informatics (code) will be published in a methodological article. Our data, both for technical and iconographic signatures, will be published in a data paper. Finally, we also aim at open source and free access in order to provide IT tools to a large public and/or to the scientific community.
