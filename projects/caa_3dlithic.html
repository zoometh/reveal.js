Object STL surface acquisition üìè

STructured light (STL) SmartScan 3D Breuckmann laser 3D acquisition
by Mill√°n Mozota, IMF-CSIC
The object scanned is an experimental peeble belonging to a wear analysis frame of reference, and prepared by Mill√°n. The accuracy of the scan is sub- mm. The RXFOR software was used to convert the point cloud into a mesh, and the OptoCat software was used to align the upper half of the scanned roller with the lower part of the scan, the RMS error was 0.006 mm.

Use-wear confocal acquisition üî¨


Confocal SensoSCAN Sensofar PLu neox 3D acquisition
by Hala Alarashi, IMF-CSIC
The confocal optics was x5, depth 3,504 μm with 293 sections. The acquisition window is 2.55 x 1.91 mm (768 x 516 px) with 'blue light' and topographic measurements. During this process, when the pebble is under blue light, photogrammetry is performed.

Confocal acquisition window relocation

SfM 3D acquisition with Meshroom or online Meshroom for GoogleColab
Meshroom is an open-source SfM software based on the AliceVision framework. Meshroom requires an NVIDIA GPU with CUDA >= 2.0 for the MVS part. However, if your computer doesn't have this capacities, you can run Meshroom online. During the process, the RMS error was under 0.01 mm. The app generates a log that can be saved
